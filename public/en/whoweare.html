Who We Are

Our story is rooted in the experience and professionalism that have always set this lighting shop apart. Under new management, we aim to continue upholding the excellence and passion for lighting that, for years, have made Benetti Illuminazione a leading name in the city and the Agno Valley region.


Today, with a fresh perspective on the latest trends in design and technology, we uphold the values of expertise, professionalism and customer care that have defined us since our inception. We offer a carefully curated selection of chandeliers, wall lights, spotlights and lighting solutions to suit every style and requirement, along with a consultancy service to help you choose the perfect lighting for your home or business.


We’re here to welcome you, offer advice and bring your projects to life with quality and professionalism. We look forward to seeing you!
