Cookie Policy


1. What are cookies


Cookies are small text files that websites save on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) while you browse. They are used to make websites work better, improve the user experience, analyze traffic, and in some cases, offer personalized content.


2. Types of cookies used by this site


a. Technical cookies (necessary)


These cookies are essential for the functioning of the site. For example, they allow:

  • Navigating between pages
  • Accessing protected areas
  • Remembering user preferences

They do not require consent.


b. Analytical cookies (statistical)


We use tools like Google Analytics to collect data in an aggregated form about the use of the site (pages visited, time spent, traffic source). These cookies can be disabled, but they help us improve the site.


c. Third-party cookies (marketing and profiling)


Services like YouTube videos, Google Maps, social buttons (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) can install cookies to track the user's activity and propose personalized content or advertisements.

They require your consent before being activated.


3. Management of consent


On first access to the site, a banner is displayed that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Personalize your preferences

You can modify or revoke your consent at any time by clicking the "[Manage cookie preferences]" button
